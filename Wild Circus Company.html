    Pirates

    The greatest and fantastic performance of Pirate Never realized, in a grandiose setting mixing Musical Comedy, waterfalls, magic, dances, juggling.
    Relive with us the fabulous history of pirates. Magicians, dancers and jugglers assisted by the exceptional presence of the stunt pirates of the extreme, never a show of pirates had such a magnitude, such amplitude.

    Fleas

    Suspended at straps attached to the Carrousel - a stage device hung above the stage - the artists make large envelopes above the audience.
    This spectacular four-dimensional demonstration requires an exemplary sense of rhythm, not to mention the skill and physical strength required to move at high speed in a 360-degree rayon.

    Virus

    In "V", the Wild Circus weaves a timeless aquatic tapestry of art, surrealism and theatrical romance. Inspired by the concept of infinity and the refined elegance of water, "V" pays tribute to the magic of the theater.
    Acrobats of international caliber, synchronized swimmers and divers offer an unusual experience.
